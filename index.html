The Shift to Clean Energy

Due to the looming economic downturn from COVID-19, the Australian government are a proposing a 'gas lead recovery' whereby they invest in new gas energy infrastructure. They argue that this is key to transitioning towards renewables and that Australian energy isn't that dirty to begin with. 79% of Australians say they care about climate change, but many are unaware of the real threat. It's not looking good for Australia so far.

Australia has the 22nd dirtiest energy in the world, putting it in the bottom 15th percentile for clear energy. When compared with some of the biggest energy users, Australia appears to be using a lot of renewables, however, it is also one of the biggest users of fossil fuels as Australians use vast amounds of electricity.

While it's clear we're still not doing well, are we at least on a downwards trajectory?
